How to use on LAN:

  1. Open this page on two different devices on the same network
  2. On both devices, click "Start Camera"
  3. On one device, click "Create Call" and copy the offer
  4. Paste the offer on the other device and click "Answer Call"
  5. Copy the answer from the second device
  6. Paste the answer on the first device and click "Set Answer"
  7. You should now be connected!
